Site Name

No Excuse Outdoors - This website hosts resources associated with the activities for people who have visual or physical disabilities.

Optional domain availability: https://www.noexcuss.com

Target Audience / Purpose

No Excuse Hunting and Outdoors is dedicated to everyone who enjoys all the outdoors has to offer regardless of a physical or visual disability. We believe that having a disability is not the same as having an inability to enjoy the outdoors. We encourage everyone to enjoy the outdoors regardless of what challenges life has put in the way. Success is not measured by harvesting an animal on a hunt or landing a fish, but by going outdoors and doing what many take for granted despite having a visual or physical disability.
